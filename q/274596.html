  • mtL convert to lowercase.
  • gkx"..." use a regex to replace broad vowels with a, slender with i, and consonant clusters with t.
  • fiw determine if it has an infix ...
  • fno(wR"ita ati") ... of either ita or ati.

Reflection

  • There should be pPX but not for regexes. That is combine pP with ah'.

How?

*3S⁼V - Link: list, A
 3    - three
*     - {A} exponentiate {3} (vectorises)
  S   - sum
    V - evaluate {A} as Jelly code -> concatenated integer *
   ⁼  - {SumOfCubes} equals {that}?

* Leading zeros produce a side effect of printing zeros before yielding the result.

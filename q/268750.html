_þ              | Outer using subtract (will implicitly turn n into 1..n)
  Ḣ             | Head
   U            | Reverse order of innermost lists
    ×_/}        | Multiply by the difference between the two points
        Z       | Transpose
         >þ/    | Outer using greater than
            ¬   | Not

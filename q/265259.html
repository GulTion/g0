The carry-less product of 3 and 3 in binary is computed as follows 1:

  1 1
x 1 1
-----
  1 1
1 1
-----
1 0 0 1

Therefore, the carry-less binary product of 3 and 3 is 1001 in binary, which is equivalent to 9 in decimal.

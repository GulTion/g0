MATL, 6 bytes

t:tY+m

Try at MATL online!

How it works

This uses the fact that tetrahedral numbers are the convolution of the natural numbers with themselves (see OEIS). It suffices to compute the convolution of the finite sequence 1, 2, ..., n.

t     % Implicit input: n. Duplicate
:     % Range: gives [1 2 ... n]
t     % Duplicate
Y+    % Convolution
m     % Ismember. Implicit output

$_=(1x$_)=~/((?|^(1)|(1\2)\1))+$/

Counts the umbrella that can cover the most beach from the left-hand end onwards, then recurses using the remaining beach to the right of that.

If b is the result of the beach soil test and l is its length, then b*2-1:l-1 gives the amount of beach covered from the left-hand end by each possible umbrella placement (which is a negative value if the umbrella can't reach the left-hand end): so we just remove the max of this from b at each step.

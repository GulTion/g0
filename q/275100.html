Method

In a nutshell:

  • we store the 5 main patterns in a 240-bit BigInt with 2 bits per character
  • we turn each of them into a matrix of 4 rows and 6 columns, filled with integers in \$[0\dots3]\$
  • the other patterns are generated by applying the following transformation 6 times:

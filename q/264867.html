Implements long addition on two lists of digits in reverse order padded to the length of the output with zeroes. Un-golfed version:

def long_addition(a, b)
  carry = 0
  a.zip(b).map {|x, y|
    sum = x + y + carry
    carry = sum > 9 ? 1 : 0
    sum % 10
  }.reverse
end

Explanation

[:+/(<0 1)|:(*|."1)    Input: matrix M
              |."1     Reverse each row of M
             *         Multiply element-wise M and the row-reversed M
    (<0 1)|:           Take the diagonal of that matrix
[:+/                   Sum that diagonal and return it=

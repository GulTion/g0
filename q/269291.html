=sumproduct(B2:B,1/3<=mod(A2:A/24+C2,1))

Paste the UTC offset and population data in cells A2:B, put the time of the day in cell C2, and the formula in cell D2.

There was some confusion whether the expected results shown in the test cases were correct. The formula above matches the updated expected results.

Spreadsheet dateserials use a system where the duration of one day is 1. Eight hours, 8:00 am, is one third of a day, i.e., 1/3, and the value of 24 hours is exactly 1.

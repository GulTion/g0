Method

The main observation here is that the following methods are equivalent:

  1. Applying the transformation to the leading digits and testing whether the result is one of the trailing digits.
  2. Applying the transformation to all digits and testing whether the result is \$0\$.

With that in mind, we define \$F\$ as:

$$F(k)=\sum_{i=0}^{10}a_i\times(k-i)$$

and we test whether we have \$F(10)\bmod11<2\$ and \$F(11)\bmod11<2\$.

Method

The main observation here is that these two algorithms are equivalent:

  1. Applying the transformation to the leading digits and testing whether the result is one of the trailing digits (the algorithm described in the challenge).
  2. Applying the transformation to all digits and testing whether the result is \$0\$.

With that in mind, we define \$F\$ as:

$$F(k)=\sum_{i=0}^{10}a_i\times(k-i)$$

and we test whether we have \$F(10)\bmod11<2\$ and \$F(11)\bmod11<2\$.
